## HOW TO GET STARTED

### Step 1: Choose Your Path
Pick ONE certificate based on:
- Your interests (creative vs. analytical vs. technical)
- Salary goals
- Job market demand
- Current skills/background

### Step 2: Enroll on Coursera
- Go to the certificate link above
- Click "Enroll for Free" or "Start 7-day Free Trial"
- **Financial Aid Available**: If $49/month is too much, apply for Coursera Financial Aid (100% free if approved)

### Step 3: Study Schedule
- Dedicate 10 hours/week (2 hours/day weekdays OR 5 hours weekend days)
- Follow course order (they build on each other)
- Complete hands-on projects (these become your portfolio)
- Don't skip assessments

### Step 4: Build Your Portfolio
As you complete projects:
- Save all your work
- Create a portfolio website (use Notion, Wix, or WordPress)
- Write case studies explaining your projects
- Add screenshots/links

### Step 5: Leverage CareerCircle
Once you graduate:
- Access exclusive job board (Google Career Certificates graduates only)
- Get resume review
- Practice mock interviews
- Connect directly with hiring companies
